Technology Uncertainty

Please click on the instructions below to read more about this step in the tool and add uncertainty.
Instructions

So far the tool has been assuming a single value for the cost and performance of each technology.

 

In this part of the tool you have two sliders that you can adjust for each technology: the uncertainty range for cost and generation. When you update each slider, it will update the uncertainty bars on the select technology. The vertical bar represents the range in cost, and the horizontal bar represents the range in generation.

Under the sliders are four scenario buttons. Each of these buttons represents an extreme scenario. When you press each of the buttons it changes the entire plot to that scenario. For example, the "Min Generation/Min Cost" updates each technology to the lowest end of the cost and generation ranges. For this scenario, users are seeing what could be a worst-case scenario. To revert back to your portfolio, press "Reset". The purpose of these scenarios and the uncertainty bars is for users to begin to consider uncertainty and engage with the implications of uncertainty on the possible success of their portfolio.
