Many of the values we use for the year 2025 are based on estimates on levelized cost of electricity provided by Lazard [CITE]. They report ranges of values for LCOE and in most cases we have chosen numbers from the middle of the range. However, you can modify the values for any of the technologies by using the sliders provided.
